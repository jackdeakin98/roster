Get Roster Data
Step 1: Go to myintegrale.rail.co.uk/rosterview.html and log in. Wait for the roster to fully load.
Step 2: Open the browser console (F12 on PC, or use the iOS Shortcut you already built). Copy and run this command:
console.log(JSON.stringify(getInfoFromStorage("RV_SaveRosterDays"))) tap to copy
Step 3: Copy the output and paste it below.
